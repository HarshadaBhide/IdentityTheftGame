Play Again!

Game for identity theft awareness

Aim - Player has to pass through correct door.

Rules - Level 1

There are true/false questions.

For each question, there are two doors GREEN (representing right answer) and RED (representing wrong answer).

Player has to choose right door to pass else they clash and game ends!

Rules - Level 2

There are multiple choice questions.

For each question, there are three doors GREEN, RED , ORANGE.

Player has to choose right door to pass else they clash and game ends!
